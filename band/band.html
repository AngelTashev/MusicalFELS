Who are we?

First English Language School’s rock band, currently called Whitelines, is a project organised by Mrs Nikolina Angelova – the director and conductor of FELS’s Brass Orchestra. Though a school band, it has the fluidity of being able to accommodate new members almost every school year in which way it stays fresh and mobile. The available instruments are two guitars, a bass guitar, and a drum set - all provided by the school.
